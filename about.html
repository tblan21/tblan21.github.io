About Me

I'm Taylor, an aspiring full-stack developer from Louisiana.

I'm a problem solver. I love to pore over a budget and ask myself, "Where could my money be better spent next month?" or to analyze a calendar and ponder, "How can I better manage my time?". I have enjoyed putting these problem-solving skills to work in both office settings and while working as a freelance writer, but now, I've decided to challenge myself-- another thing I love to do-- and push my problem-solving abilities to their limit by pivoting into full-stack web development.

As a result, I'm learning that I love applying creativity to problem-solving and logic more than I even realized! For example, now, I get to ask myself: "Hmm, the text on my index.html page is aligned center, but the text on my about.html page is not. What's different in the code between these two pages?". I find this satisfying!

I'm excited to put my development skills to use to build smooth, efficient systems, websites, and applications that serve the world.

When I'm not coding, you can find me:

  • Spending time outside
  • Listening to chillstep
  • Hanging out with my rescue Pitbull, Rhiannon
